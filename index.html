ECOMMERCE MIGRATIONS & BUILDS FAQS

What is the best CMS for an ecommerce website?

We recommend Shopify for all ecommerce website builds. As Shopify Partners, we can vouch for Shopify’s capabilities of being a reliable, secure, and scalable platform. Whether you’re a startup or enterprise-level business, Shopify can be tailored to your needs. Shopify allows for custom design and development, customization within ecommerce processes like shipping and fulfillment, and is easy for store owners and content managers to update as needed. Shopify offers unbeatable hosting and maintenance service, and we’ve seen many stores from all types of industries successfully grow within the platform.

How much is a Shopify website?

Every Shopify website has unique requirements, expectations, outputs, creative solutions to meet brand goals. Because we do not use pre-made templates, our Shopify experts must understand these factors first, in order to create a customized Shopify website that’s tailored to maximize your return on investment.

How long does it take to see results for a new Shopify website?

Since our Shopify websites will restructure or modify your ecommerce SEO strategy, it can take 60-90 days for Google’s algorithm to learn all the changes. This is when we will see the biggest improvement. However, with the launch of a new custom Shopify website, you'll notice an immediate increase in performance such as page speed, search visibility, organic traffic, and conversion rates.

Do you offer any guarantees?

With almost a decade of Shopify experience, we have a proven track record of success for winning results. One of the most important phases of a website launch is what happens after. Monitoring site performance and web analytics can offer insights into maximizing your new website’s fullest potential. This is why we offer (60) days of SEO maintenance, including: a review of performance and next steps, making proposed adjustments, and advanced reporting tools.
